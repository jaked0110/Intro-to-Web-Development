Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to sign up or create an account?
Nope. OnAir is completely account-free. Just browse by mood or genre and start listening — it’s that simple.
Can I request songs or submit my own music?
Yes! Independent artists and sound engineers can submit music via our Contact Form. If it fits a channel, we’ll feature it.
Will the music change or update over time?
Yes — we regularly refresh and rotate tracks in each channel to keep the experience fresh, while maintaining the mood and flow you expect.
Why can’t I skip tracks or choose specific songs?
OnAir is designed as a streaming radio experience, not an on-demand player.
